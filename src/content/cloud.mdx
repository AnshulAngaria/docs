[Okteto Cloud](https://cloud.okteto.com/#/?origin=docs) gives you free access to secure Kubernetes namespaces, fully integrated with remote development capabilities.
Develop your Kubernetes applications in Okteto Cloud and forget about slow and tedious local development forever.

With Okteto Cloud you can:

- Create [secure Kubernetes namespaces](/docs/cloud/multitenancy/) with one click
- Deploy your applications [from Git repositories](/docs/cloud/deploy-from-git/), [Docker Compose](/docs/tutorials/stacks-getting-started/) files or [Helm charts](/docs/cloud/deploy-from-helm/)
- Visualize your workloads in a Developer-focused Kubernetes dashboard
- Get [automatic HTTPs endpoints](/docs/cloud/ssl/) for your services
- Configure [secrets](/docs/cloud/secrets/) and prevent secure credentials from being stored in version control
- [Download kubectl credentials](/docs/cloud/credentials/) to access your namespaces with `kubectl`, `helm`, or any other CLI tool
- Develop directly in Okteto Cloud with the [Okteto CLI](https://github.com/okteto/okteto)
- Build, push, and redeploy your applications with [Okteto Push](https://okteto.com/blog/okteto-push-code-to-kubernetes-in-seconds/)
- Collaborate by [sharing](/docs/cloud/namespaces/#share-your-namespace) your namespaces with your teammates

Head over to our [5 minutes getting started guide](/docs/getting-started/) and start developing the cloud-native way today!
